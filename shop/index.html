Angled Keyboard: It's quick to get the keyboard out of the way, yet keep it in easy reach. Choose the angled parking position if you want to reduce dust collection or inadvertent key presses by your pets or kids.

Keyboard: Choose the flat parking position when you want to use your keyboard even when parked. See "Wacom" tab for an example. Parking and retrieving is just as fast as with the angled position.

Laptop: Dual display when in use, lots of space when parked. Shown: a 32" display with a 13" laptop. Smaller displays will accommodate larger laptops. If you want to use a full-sized keyboard, ViDock-One offers two other laptop configurations.

Tablet: Dual display when in use, lots of space when parked. Shown: a 32" display with a 12" tablet. Smaller displays will accommodate larger tablets.

Wacom: Ever felt cramped when drawing on a tablet while still needing your keyboard for shortcuts? ViDock-One's bay comfortably makes space for both. When not needing your tablet you can park it in easy reach, with and without the keyboard.

Competent Support

All our support staff are in-house and part of our development team. You post your support request on our Facebook page or our ticket system and we typically get back to you in under two working days.

Our customers voted against using a service center that offers faster responses, but with the down side of using agents who do not know the product.

Local

We have local warehouses in Las Vegas, Hamburg and Guangzhou. This means for USA and European customers the product is already imported, all customs and import tax paid. Low shipping costs, too.

Warranty & Returns

We guarantee our products to last 50 years, backed up by a 10 year warranty. In the rare event that you must repair or return the product we make the process straight forward.

Buying ViDock funds V²

Saving your money and our planet's resources when upgrading your computer is our mission at the V² Movement. All our work is funded through the ViDock / V² community by buying ViDock — or by inspiring people to buy ViDock. The development of ViDock-One was completely funded by ViDock-eGPU sales and donations. ViDock-One sales will fund the development of an open source Modular-in-One™ standard. That is an All-in-One form factor that is made for people and manufacturers, not for shareholder value.

Modules in Community Development

ViDock-One not only comes with four modules, additional four modules are on the way:

ViDock-Lift

Bring ergonomic efficiency to your workspace, elevating your view of your screen while at the same time clearing your desk by securing your computer cables and dongles under the notebook platform.

Catch-all

A distinctive home for all your desk items. The cup is precision CNC cut from beech hardwood and hand finished with vegetable oil. It holds large items like pencils, phone, mouse, etc.

Speaker 2.0

If you are looking for a marked improvement in sound quality over built-in notebook or display speakers, yet you do not want to sacrifice simplicity, elegance and portability, then look no further.

Speaker 2.1

All the goodness of the Speaker 2.0, with the added oomph of a subwoofer. This speaker enclosure harnesses the superior acoustic properties of solid beech hardwood.
