Basic Setup: A setup like this will be sufficient in most use case. It is made only using parts from ViDock-Plate-Kit and is not using any ViDock accessories. We have successfully built setups (w/o accessories) for routers, mini-PCs, all cable and dongle clutter for desktops. For your reference, the size of the display pictured is 24".

Open-Standard: V²Dock is the Open-Standard (and compatible with the existing pegboard industry standard) to which the product ViDock-Plate is compliant. Your instant benefits are that we do not lock you into our system, rather you are free to buy more fasteners or compatible pegboard accessories in any hardware store. Your future benefits are that other manufacturers may offer additional products that will be compatible with your ViDock-Plate.

Tips: Larger devices than the depicted Intel NUC mini PC, such as a Mac mini, Alienware Alpha, or big routers will also fit. You can mount them so that part of the device extends beyond the plate. In this case cables are tied up to the side or behind of ViDock-Plate. Please note the disclaimer on Balance in the specs.

Parked Headset: Headsets are great in a conference or for gaming, but otherwise they can get in the way. Use one of the two hooks as a headset holder. Get your headset off your desk, out of sight, yet within easy reach!

Power-Bricks: Nowadays most mini-PCs and display come with power bricks that clutter either your desk our foot space. Mount them securely either with two V²Dock-Ties.

V²Dock-Ties™: A robust mounting system comprised of zip ties of varied sizes ranging from super tiny to really long. Suitable for mounting anything from tiny objects up to large dongles such as hubs or power strips. Remounting is easy, as large ones are releasable, small ones are plentiful. Note: By placing rubber bumpers (of the V²Dock-Protectors) under your dongle just one tie will fasten it securely to the plate.

V²Dock-Parking™: Simple, yet effective solution to get items out of the way, yet keeps them within easy reach. Use the hook versus ties for items you need to retrieve frequently, such as headsets, notebook's power supply or charging cables. Also see → V²Dock-Ring and → V²Dock-Hook.

V²Dock-Hook™: Straight forward: Place it where you need it on the plate and hang things from it. So useful, we give you two.

V²Dock-Ring™: The rings will get items hooked that don't let you otherwise. Examples are notebook power supplies or charging cables. Use the V²Dock-Ties to attach the ring to your item. So versatile and useful, we give you many rings (…to hang them all!).

V²Dock-Pegs™: Used to hold large and heavy devices, such as the mini-PC. Used in conjunction with the V²Dock-Strap.

Power Strip: The root of your cable mess. Uplift your spirits by uprooting your power strip and tying it to the plate. Use two V²Dock-Ties or one tie is sufficient if you place the rubber bumpers (included with V²Dock-Protectors) under the strip.

V²Dock-Strap™: 10mm wide hook and loop fastener nylon straps. Use the soft side facing your device to protect its surface. Customize the 2m roll to length. Use it for large devices where V²Dock-Ties are too short. Note: Use at least 10cm fastening overlap for safety!

Mini-PCs & Routers: Resting on V²Dock-Pegs and securely fastened with V²Dock-Straps. For size and weight constraints refer to specs.

Cables: During assembly use the V²Dock-Hooks to collect all cables. When done tie them securely to the side or behind of V²Dock-Plate using V²Dock-Ties.

V²Dock-Plate™: The heart of this system. Made of 2mm strong aircraft grade aluminum, hairline finish for good looks and anodized for scratch resistance. The 25mm hole pattern is compatible with VESA mount hole patterns and 1" peg board accessories. The mounting holes are used for V²Dock-Hook, V²Dock-Ties, V²Dock-Strap.

V²Dock-Protectors™: Always use protection when mounting devices with nice surfaces, or ones that vibrate, or when tying items with just one tie. We provide you with a selection of pads and bumpers.

Why create an Open-Standard?

We are community developers. Our mission is to improve your life and save your money. An Open-Standard enables any manufacturer to build compatible products and accessories. In turn you are not locked into our products, rather we free you to buy from other brands, effectively reducing eco waste and saving your cash.

Can I change computer type?

Absolutely! The computer mount is so versatile that it works with any computer as long as you stay within size or weight constraints!

Can I change display type?

Absolutely! As long as you stay within maximum dimensions or weight constraints.

What does 50 Year Guarantee mean?

Simply put: With proper care ViDock-Plate-Kit will work through your computer and display generations and for your lifetime.

How not to lose the thingamajigs?

Store all the fasteners in the small product box which fits nicely on any book shelf.

What if I need new fasteners?

All fasteners are common parts, available at good hardware stores.

How mature is the product?

Short answer: Very. Long answer: We have been developing and more importantly testing this product for years in more revisions than we want to count with a diverse team of developers, testers and reviewers.
